2.2 Preparing features, labels and models (16%)

Based on our variance, correlation analysis and visualization, we have conclude that drop MEM, VSIZE, MINFLT for memory activity and drop CPU, TRUN, TSLPU, PRI, RTPR, CPUNR, EXC for process activity. All reamining feature goes into feature selection for our machine learning model

In order to perfrom a classification model, we need some form of feature engineering. First, we drop the irrelvant columns and get the new dataset with relevant columns. Then we split the numer columns and category columns into 2 seperate variable. And mark our traget variable as 'attack' and then we apply stringIndexer, OneHotEncoder and vector Assembler to combine the categoric and numeric columne into our feature. after that, we create our algorithms on decision tree or GBTClassifier and fit all the steps into a pipeline.

And finally, we pass the train dataset into the pipeline to train the model and further test our test dataset to validate the performance.
